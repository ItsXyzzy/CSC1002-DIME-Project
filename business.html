Revenue Model

PAM AI uses a freemium model. The free plan gets users through the door, and PAM Pro offers advanced features for those who want more control over their finances.

Growth Roadmap

PAM AI launches in Ireland and expands across Europe over three years, using Ireland's EU membership to access all 27 member states.

Year 1 — Ireland Launch

PAM Standard launches as a free app targeting Irish students and young professionals. We focus on connecting with AIB, Bank of Ireland, and Revolut through Open Banking. The goal is to build a strong user base and improve the AI using real spending data.

Target: 10,000 active users

Year 2 — Monetisation

Once users see real savings from PAM's insights, we convert them to PAM Pro. We also explore partnerships with Irish credit unions and introduce contextual affiliate revenue from financial products recommended by the AI.

Target: €500,000 annual recurring revenue

Year 3+ — EU Expansion

Using Ireland's EU financial passporting rights, PAM expands into Germany, France, and the Netherlands the largest retail banking markets in the EU. The same product, localised for each country, with regional bank integrations added gradually.

Target: 500,000 users across the EU
