What is PAM AI?

PAM AI is a next-generation personal account manager designed specifically for the Irish market. By leveraging large language models (LLMs), PAM transforms your financial data into personalized insights and actionable advice, helping you take control of your money like never before.

💡

AI-Powered Insights

Our LLMs analyze your transaction data to provide personalized financial insights, helping you understand your spending habits and identify opportunities to save.

🔗

Unified Dashboard

Connect all your Irish bank accounts in one secure place. PAM aggregates your financial data, giving you a comprehensive view of your finances without the hassle of logging into multiple platforms.

📊

Real-Time Tracking

Get real-time updates on your spending and receive instant notifications about unusual activity or upcoming bills, so you can stay on top of your finances effortlessly.

🔒

Bank-Level Security

Your data security is our top priority. PAM uses advanced encryption and secure authentication methods to ensure your financial information is protected at all times.

Who is PAM AI for?

We’ve built PAM for the modern Irish financial landscape—targeting users who are underserved by traditional, fragmented banking apps.

📱

The Multi-Banker

Young professionals balancing a traditional pillar bank (BOI/AIB) with digital-first platforms like Revolut and N26.

🎓

The Gen-Z Saver

Students and early-career starters who want a conversational, jargon-free interface that speaks their language.

✂️

The Subscription-Heavy

Users struggling to track multiple streaming services and "free trials" that have turned into hidden monthly drains.

Our Business Strategy

PAM AI is committed to revolutionizing personal finance management in Ireland through three key pillars:

  • Customer-Centric Design: Prioritizing intuitive, accessible UX tailored for Irish consumers.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Leveraging LLMs to turn raw data into actionable financial intelligence.
  • Strategic Partnerships: Collaborating with Irish banks to expand reach and utility.
