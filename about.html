Unique Value Proposition

What is PAM AI?

A next-generation personal account manager built for the Irish market. PAM uses large language models to turn your financial data into personalised insights and actionable advice.

💡

AI-Powered Insights

Our LLMs analyse your transactions to surface personalised insights that help you understand spending habits and find opportunities to save.

🔗

Unified Dashboard

Connect all your Irish bank accounts in one secure place. No more logging into AIB, BOI, and Revolut separately to check your balance.

📊

Real-Time Tracking

Instant notifications on unusual activity and upcoming bills, so you're never caught off guard.

🔒

Bank-Level Security

Advanced encryption and secure authentication ensure your financial data is protected at all times.

Target Audience

Who is PAM AI for?

Built for the modern Irish financial landscape, targeting users underserved by traditional, fragmented banking apps.

📱

The Multi-Banker

A study by Accenture showed that over 80% of Irish consumers use multiple banking platforms, leading to fragmented financial management.

🎓

The Gen-Z Saver

Students and early-career starters who want a conversational, modern, jargon-free interface that speaks their language.

✂️

The Subscription-Heavy

Users struggling to track multiple streaming services and "free trials" that have quietly become monthly drains.

Business Model

Our Strategy

PAM AI is built on three core pillars that drive our approach to the Irish fintech market.

  • Customer-Centric Design Prioritising intuitive, accessible UX tailored for Irish consumers not financial experts.
  • Data-Driven Insights Leveraging LLMs to turn raw transaction data into actionable financial intelligence.
  • Strategic Partnerships Collaborating with Irish banks to expand reach, utility, and referral revenue.
