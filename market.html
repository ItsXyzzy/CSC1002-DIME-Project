Our Target Market

PAM AI targets individuals at different stages of financial confidence, with a strong focus on behavioral needs rather than just demographics. Our primary market includes young adults entering financial independence, professionals struggling with cash flow, and users who experience tress or avoidance when managing money. By combining intelligent insights with a supportive interface, PAM AI helps users build confidence and take control of their financial habits.

User Personas

Student / Young Adult

Aged 18–24 and new to managing money. Needs simple guidance on “needs vs. wants” and help building good financial habits.

The Paycheck-to-Paycheck Professional

Earns regularly but struggles with cash flow. Needs alerts and insights to avoid overspending and stay on budget

The Anxious Avoider

Avoids finances due to stress. Needs a simple, supportive experience that makes managing money feel less overwhelming.
